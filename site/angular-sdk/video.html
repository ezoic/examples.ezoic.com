What this demonstrates

The SDK offers two video surfaces. <ezoic-video-embed> injects the Open Video script and mounts a floating, autoplaying player into its own host element; <ezoic-video> emits a bare div the Ezoic video runtime discovers and fills, with a publisher-chosen div id.
Integration used on this page
<!-- video.component.ts template -->
<ezoic-video-embed videoId="demo-video-id" playlist="demo-playlist" float autoplay />
<ezoic-video divId="demo-video-slot-1" />
